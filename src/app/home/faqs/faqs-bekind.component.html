FAQs Frequently asked questions

What is the BeKind program?

BeKind is a school-based wellbeing and kindness initiative designed to help students grow in empathy, social responsibility, and positive behaviour through simple, practical acts of kindness.

Each week, students focus on a specific “Act of Kindness” such as speaking encouragement, listening well, helping others, or honouring local heroes. Teachers introduce the theme through lessons and activities, and students put kindness into action within their school and community.

Schools receive access to a digital portal where teachers can track participation, celebrate progress, and view reflections or stories shared by students (teacher-moderated).

Who can take part?

The program is suitable for:

  • primary and secondary students
  • teachers and wellbeing teams
  • whole-school implementations or individual classes
  • school leadership looking to strengthen wellbeing culture

BeKind is designed to be flexible for any Australian school.

How long does the program run for?

BeKind is designed to be run over 5 consecutive weeks, focusing on one act of kindness each week. However, a school can opt to run it over 10 weeks, focusing on one act of kindness over two weeks instead of the one. Teachers are welcome to adjust what time of year/term to suit their class or school calendar.

Can we choose which acts of kindness to focus on?

The acts of kindness are pre set.

  • Cheer a Champ - BeKind through speaking
  • Love the Lonely - BeKind through Listening
  • Sharing is Caring - BeKind through Giving
  • Honour a Hero - BeKind through Thanking
  • Serve a City - BeKind through Serving

What do we receive when we sign up?

Schools and teachers gain access to:

  • A dedicated school or class dashboard for tracking participation
  • Lesson plans aligned with the Australian Curriculum
  • Printable worksheets and activity guides
  • Short videos introducing each Act of Kindness
  • Posters, displays, and digital graphics
  • Teacher guides and implementation support

Everything is designed to be simple, age-appropriate, and easy to run.

Do you provide teacher training?

Yes. We provide a short online training session (approx. 20–30 minutes) for teachers and wellbeing staff. This covers how to run BeKind, how to use the digital platform, and how to integrate the program into everyday school routines.

We are also available for in person teacher training, seminars or school talks upon request.
