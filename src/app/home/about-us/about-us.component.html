Our Story

The Love is Our Weapon Campaign began in a local youth ministry in Australia with a simple but powerful vision: to equip students to share God’s love in practical ways with their unsaved friends at school. What started as a small idea quickly turned into a movement.

The goal was bold: 2,000 acts of love recorded over 10 weeks. But within just 10 days, that goal was shattered. Thousands of acts of kindness poured in, stories of transformation emerged, and lives were being impacted across the region. By the end of the 10-week campaign, the youth ministry had grown dramatically, with countless testimonies and a wave of momentum that exceeded all expectations.

Our Vision

Love is our Weapon is more than a campaign: it’s a movement. It’s a bold, Spirit-empowered initiative equipping churches and believers to impact their communities through practical demonstrations of God’s love.

We envision a mobilised army of everyday people, courageously carrying the love of Jesus into streets, cities, and nations, bringing hope, healing, and transformation wherever they go.

We see lives changed and churches filled as radical, love-driven believers reach others through simple acts of kindness, empowered by the Holy Spirit.

We see churches renewed, unified in purpose, ignited with compassion, and focused on mission.

We see lasting fruit, families restored, communities transformed, and the world marked by the power of God’s love.
