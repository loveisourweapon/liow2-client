Our Story

The Love is our Weapon Campaign started in a local youth ministry in Australia. The youth ministry wanted to find a way to help empower students to better reach out to the unsaved in their schools. From this need, the idea for the campaign was birthed.

The youth ministry had a goal to see 2,000 acts of love registered on their website over 10 weeks. Instead they saw 2,000 acts of love registered in the first 10 days. By the end of the 10 week campaign, many thousands of deeds had been registered on the website, dozens of testimonies came forth from the lives impacted and the youth ministry doubled in size.
