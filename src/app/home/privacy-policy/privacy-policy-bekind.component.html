Privacy Policy

Introduction

BeKind partners with schools, teachers, and students to promote positive behaviour and wellbeing. We value the trust that school communities place in us, and we are committed to working in a transparent, ethical, responsible, and secure manner.

BeKind complies with relevant Australian privacy laws, including the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the Australian Privacy Principles, and applicable Department of Education privacy policies and guidelines.

We are committed to protecting personal information and being clear about what we collect, how we use it, and the choices schools and families have. This applies whether you are a student, teacher, parent/carer, or a visitor to our website or digital platforms.

This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal information provided to us online, in person, through digital sign-ups, or via school-managed systems. It also outlines how we handle information obtained from third parties when relevant (for example, school administration systems or Department of Education platforms).

If you engage with BeKind—through our website, digital resources, learning activities, or school programs—we will make this policy available so you understand how your information is handled.

Privacy Policy Summary

It is important to read the full policy for complete detail. The key points are:

What We Collect

  • We collect personal information only where necessary for delivering the BeKind school program. This may include a student or teacher’s name, school, class, email address (for teachers only), and online identifiers such as IP address or device information.
  • We do not collect more information than is required, and for students, all data collection occurs with school oversight and in alignment with Department of Education policies.

Who We Collect Information From

We collect information from individuals involved in the BeKind program, including:

  • teachers and school staff who register or manage the program
  • students participating in BeKind activities (through teacher-managed platforms)
  • parents/carers when required for consent
  • visitors to our website or digital platforms

Why We Collect This Information

We collect information to:

  • provide access to BeKind learning resources and activities
  • support teachers in delivering the program
  • improve program quality through anonymised usage insights
  • ensure safe and appropriate participation by students
  • meet administrative, reporting, and compliance requirements

We may also use anonymised, aggregated data for program evaluation, research, and improvement.

What We Don’t Do

  • We never sell personal information.
  • We never share identifying information with other organisations for their marketing or promotional purposes.
  • We do not use student data for advertising, profiling, or commercial gain.

When We Share Information

We only share personal information:

  • when required by law
  • with trusted service providers who support the delivery of the BeKind program (e.g., secure digital learning providers)
  • under strict agreements ensuring data security, limited use, and compliance with Department privacy requirements

How We Protect Information

We take all reasonable steps to keep personal information secure, including:

  • secure storage systems
  • restricted access
  • encryption
  • compliance monitoring

Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to:

  • the BeKind website and digital learning platforms
  • communications such as emails or digital notifications sent to teachers or schools
  • any data collected as part of school participation in the BeKind program

It outlines what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, what we won’t do with it, and the rights schools, parents/carers, and students have.
