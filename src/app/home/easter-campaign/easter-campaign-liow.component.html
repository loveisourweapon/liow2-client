The Easter Campaign is a five-week evangelistic campaign of practical love that leads into a gospel presentation at your Easter 2026 service.

From 1st March to 5th April, churches unite around four simple acts of love that open hearts to Jesus:

Week 1: Cheer a Champ

Celebrate someone who needs encouragement and speak life into them.

Week 2: Love the Lonely

Reach out to someone who feels unseen and show them genuine care.

Week 3: Sharing Is Caring

Bless someone with generosity in a practical, meaningful way.

Week 4: Pray for Your Peeps

Pray for friends, family, neighbours and believe for open doors to invite them.

Week 5: Save a Soul

These four expressions of love above create natural moments that lead beautifully into the gospel and opportunities for invitation to your Easter service.
